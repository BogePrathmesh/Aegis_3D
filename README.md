# AEGIS-3D
## AI-Powered Structural Health Monitoring & Budget Optimization System

AEGIS-3D is an AI-based infrastructure inspection and decision-support platform that detects structural cracks, predicts future deterioration, estimates structural risk, and recommends which structures should be repaired within a limited budget to reduce maximum failure risk.

The system combines **Computer Vision, Depth Estimation, Structural Risk Modeling, Degradation Simulation, and Budget Optimization** to help move infrastructure maintenance from reactive repair to predictive and risk-based planning.

---

## Key Features

- Crack Detection from inspection images  
- Crack Depth Estimation using depth maps  
- Structural Health Score & Risk Score  
- Failure Probability Prediction  
- Structural Degradation Simulation (future damage over time)  
- Repair Impact Analysis (risk reduction after repair)  
- AI Budget Optimization (which structures to repair within budget)  
- Dashboard with visual simulation and reports  

> Detect → Predict → Simulate → Decide
